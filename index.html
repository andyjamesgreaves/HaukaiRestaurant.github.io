Welcome to The Haukai Restaurant

The Haukai restaurant offers a local Maori cultural experience with its history and cuisine.

It offers both Maori and Pakeha kai where diners can experience te ao Maori with the use of karakia for blessing of the food.

We offer a warm friendly and intimate ambience with caring service to enhance your experience.

The Haukai restaurant is in the quaint township of Kerikeri which is nestled just inland from the beautiful Bay of Islands.

Our restaurant is actually near one of the sites where the Lord of the rings was filmed, which is our little claim to fame.

All are welcome to our humble abode and we hope you enjoy your experience with us.
